Digital Analytics and Privacy

A word about digital analytics

Analytics are primarily a business necessity. The need to know the history of your users when they come into contact with your company.

It is a story represented quantitatively, and as valid as the questions that created this story.

Why a digital analytics advisor

A digital analytics consultant aligns digital presence with business goals.

This means finding out what information should be collected and how it can be applied to opportunities and outcomes. Above all, it is a discovery process of identifying the questions that the project must answer and the best technical solutions to be able to answer.

How a digital analytics project run

A digital analytics project develops in:

  • Audit and definition of requirements
  • Architecture of the solution
  • Implementation
  • Reporting

Benefits of a digital analytics project

Coming to a solid measurement strategy means understanding what you are trying to do, how you will accomplish it, and what experience you are offering to customers. Solidity in the digital analytics solution also means supporting the project in a sustainable way towards new challenges such as migration, the introduction of new privacy laws, the need for subsequent additions.

By tapping into different disciplines, we make your analytics strategy meaningful, creating an effective way to set the right goals and turn them into an action plan.

The new GA4

GA4 is the new Google solution that will replace Universal Google Analytics. From 1 July 2023 these properties will no longer collect data and you will need to have already migrated your analytics stack to Universal Google Analytics. For YoY analytics, this migration should take off right away, for a midsize project that includes:

  • Audit and definition of requirements
  • Architecture of the solution
  • Implementation
  • Reporting
