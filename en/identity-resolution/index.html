Identity resolution

Identity resolution is the process of matching identifiers between multiple devices and contact points towards a single profile. It helps create a consistent and omnichannel vision of a consumer, allowing companies to unify data for digital marketing analysis and to optimize campaigns throughout the user journey.

With the decline of cookies it is important, with respect for the privacy of users, to combine digital entities with identifying traits such as an email or a telephone number.

Identity resolution is the only alternative to cookies, especially when mobile is the first most important device and the identifier produced by a CDP will rely on devices instead of browsers. A more robust solution than cookies also discussed by their inventor.

Why identity resolution matters

Clarify the journey. One of the biggest challenges is getting a unified view of your users by considering:

  • The same person uses multiple devices -> separation of identifiers
  • The journey develops over several channels -> data separation

Identity resolution links the anonymous identifiers of a person who interacted with digital properties via apps, smartphones, tablets and desktops, allowing you to manage communication campaigns using proprietary data, across multiple devices and multiple channels.

How a projects work

Identity resolution is part of a customer data platform project and for its potential it deserves to be illustrated separately.

In the development of a CDP as a Segment, particular attention is paid to the traits that define a person’s identification graph. For greater security and attention to privacy, the recommended approach is the deterministic one, based only on first-party information, as opposed to the probabilistic one, based on traits that can most likely connect identifiers to a particular user.

Once implemented, the customer data platform will recognize users in real time and allow for real-time personas and journeys.

Benefits of the identity resolution

With identity resolution enabled, a company will have a holistic view of user engagement. Above all, he will have the opportunity to design journeys and optimize campaigns on first-party data.
